projects

nl interface to sql

developing a system to predict sql queries from nl input. the performance of the system will be evalauated using the wikisql dataset. i am solving this problem via slot filling approach. basically divide the problem into subtasks and then conquer them all one by one. more details to follow.

cash problem via rl

cash problem has been a thoroughly researched problem and folks behind auto-sklearn are pretty much at the forefront. in their original 2015 nips paper they used bayesian optimization armed with meta-features to predict the whole pipline. i want to examine if their approach can be augmented or molded one way or the other to further improve the performance.
